FAQs

Is it allowed to use my phone?

You need to leave your phone in a locker

What happens if I'm late?

You should arrive about 15 min early for background story and get ready for the game, if you're late then you'll have less than 60min to escape

What if I NEED to get out?

You'll have a panic button, if you press it you can get out instantly, but you're not allowed to enter again, your friends can keep playing

Do I need to be in good physical conditions?

It's not necessary, you won't need strength or any kind of ressistance

What happens if I can't escape?

When time is up, the staff will open the doors, they explain to you how far you were from escaping, and let you get out
